Common Questions
What exactly is PriceHawk?
PriceHawk is a digital browser extension — a piece of software that installs directly into Google Chrome. It automatically compares product prices across Amazon, eBay, and Walmart whenever you're shopping online.
How is Premium delivered?
PriceHawk Premium is a digital software subscription delivered instantly. Upon payment, your account is automatically upgraded and all premium features are unlocked immediately within the browser extension. No download, no shipping, no waiting.
Do I need an account to use PriceHawk?
No account is needed for the free tier. An account is only required when upgrading to Premium so we can manage your subscription.
Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.
What is your refund policy?

How does the 3 searches per day limit work?
Free users can run 3 price comparisons per day. The count resets at midnight. Upgrading to Premium removes the limit entirely.

How it works

Three steps.
Best price.

PriceHawk is a Chrome browser extension — a piece of digital software that installs directly into your browser in seconds.

01
Install the extension

Add PriceHawk to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store. It's a digital software extension — free to install, no account required.

02
Browse normally

Shop on Amazon, eBay, or Walmart like you always do. Click the PriceHawk icon when you find something you want.

03
See the best price

A popup instantly shows the same product across all retailers, sorted cheapest to most expensive. Click and buy.
